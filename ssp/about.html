About This Demo

This is an example site built with Finding Your Neighborhood.

This site was created to demonstrate what a FYN door looks like when built with multiple pages and link groups. It is not a real SSP resource site and is not affiliated with any zoo, conservation organization, or AZA program. Finding Your Neighborhood is a free, open-source static site generator for intentional community doors. It does not collect visitor data, track behavior, or maintain any records. The creator defines the links. That is the whole mechanism. To build your own FYN door, visit easyfyn.com.
