We meet the second Tuesday of every month. We read one book at a time, we actually finish them, and we argue about them in good faith. We are not a social club that happens to have books. The books are the point. If you found this page, someone probably sent you here. That means they think you might belong. We tend to agree with that kind of recommendation.
